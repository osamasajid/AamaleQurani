SAFETY FROM PUNISHMENT IN THE GRAVE
Significance:
Anyone who recites
Surah Al-Mulk
daily will be
safeguarded against the punishment of the grave.
AWAKENING IN THE NIGHT
A.
And remember the time when We made The House
a place of worship for the people and a place of safety.
And take the Maqam Ibrahim as a place of performing Salaah.
And We decreed to Ibrahim and Ismaâ€™il that they should purify
My House for those who observe Tawaaf of it and those
who observe Ruku and those who observe Sajdah (in prayers)
Al Baqarah 125
Significance:
It was found written by a certain sage, that anyone who
recites the above verse before retiring to bed, he will be able to get up
at any time he desires.
