IMAM AWZA’I R.A. AND THE EVIL JINN
I seek Allah’s refuge from the devil accursed.
Significance:
Imam Awza’i R.A. is reported to have said that once an
evil Jinn suddenly confronted him, frightening him tremendously. He
immediately recited the above ayah. The Jinn escaped, crying: “You
have sought refuge from The Great! You have sought refuge from The
Great!”
