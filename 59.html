B. Surah Al-Mulk
Significance:
Surah Al-Mulk
should be recited thrice each day for 3
consecutive days. After reciting it 3 times blow on the patient’s eyes.
The suffering will be alleviated —
Insha Allah
.
STRENGTHENING THE EYESIGHT
A.
Now We have removed from you your veil and
this day your sight is iron.
Qaf 22
Significance:
Recite this verse thrice after every
salaah
, blow on the
fingers and rub them on the eyes. By doing this the eyesight will never
weaken. In fact, any weakness will also disappear.
B.
No doubt, We sent down this blessed Qur’an in Night of Power.
