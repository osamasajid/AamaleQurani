STEADFASTNESS OF THE HEART
Be firm (upon the Right Path) just as You had been commanded
and those who had repented with You, (had been commanded).
Hud 112
Significance:
For steadfastness of the heart, recite 11 times after every
Salaah
.

REMOVING SUSPICION AND DOUBT
My Lord! I seek your protection from the prompting
of the Devils; and my Lord!
I seek your protection
from them approaching me.
Al Muminun 97,98
Significance:
The excessive recitation of the above verses removes all
suspicion and doubt.
