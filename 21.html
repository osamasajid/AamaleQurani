Have not those who disbelieve known that the
heavens and the earth were of one piece,
then We parted them,and We made every living thing of water?
Will they not then believe?
Al Ambiya 30
Significance:
When a woman is in the throes of labour, the above
verse
should be recited by someone (female or mahram male) and
then
blown
on the stomach or back of the woman.
PROTECTING CHILDREN AGAINST INFANTILE DISEASES
Lo! have put my trust in Allah, My Lord and your Lord.
There is not an animal but He (Allah) grasps it by its forelock!
Lo! My Lord is on the Straight Path.
Hud 56
Significance:
To protect a child from such sicknesses which normally
afflict children, the above
ayah
should be recited and blown on the
child.
