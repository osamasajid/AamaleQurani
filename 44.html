Allah enjoins adl (justice and worshipping none
but Allah alone) and ihsaan (to be patient in
performing your duties to Allah,
totally for Allah's sake and in accordance with sunnah)
and giving help to kith and kin
(i.e. to give them financial assistance,
visiting them, caring for them)
and He forbids you from fahshaa
(sins, evil deeds, illicit sex, disobedience to parents, lies, etc.)
and from munkar (all that is prohibited by the shari'ah)
and from baghya (oppression of all kinds).
He admonishes you so you may take heed.
