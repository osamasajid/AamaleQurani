WHEN BITTEN BY A POISONOUS INSECT OR SNAKE
And when you grasp, you grasp like
men with absolute power.
Ash Shu’ara 130
Significance:
Circulate the finger around the bitten area and recite the
above verse seven times in one breath. The patient will recover shortly
— Insha Allah.
