Allah explained to us how to overcome Satan and his followers. When
a Muslim is about to commit a sin, or when he is maddened with anger,
he should say:
“I ask Allah for His refuge against the accursed Satan.”
Then he should refrain himself from committing the sin and calm his
anger. The Muslim should know that malicious incentives are always
motivated by the Satan.
