DISOBEDIENT CHILDREN
And (O Allah), be gracious towards me in
the matter of my off-spring.
And surely, I have returned to You in repentance,
and surely, I am of those who surrender (to You).
Al Ahqaf 15
Significance:
Anyone whose children are disobedient should recite
the above
Du’a
after every
Salaah
.
Insha Allah
they will soon become
obedient. But have in mind one’s children when saying the word
Dhurriyyati.
FOR INSUBORDINATION AND DISOBEDIENCE
Lo! I have put my trust in Allah
who is my Lord and your Lord.
There is no animal but He grasp it by its forelock.
Verily my Lord is on the straight path.
Hud 56
Significance:
If a person is guilty of insubordination, his/her forelock
should be grasped and the above
ayah
be recited 3 times. Thereafter
he/she should be “blown” on. By doing this,
Insha Allah
, he/she will
become obedient, as in accordance to
Shariah.
