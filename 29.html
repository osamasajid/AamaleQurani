And (O My Apostle!) when you recite the Quran,
We set up (to intervene) between you and those
who believe not in the Hereafter a hidden
veil invisible to the eye And We put coverings on their
hearts that they may not understand it,
and (cause) a heaviness in their ears.
And when you mention your Lord alone in the Quran,
they flee away turning on their back in aversion (from the Truth)
Bani Israil 45,46
Significance:
The above
verses
should be recited and blown on any
person who is frightened, shocked or terrified. These verses are very
effective in removing horrifying thoughts as well.
